About Me

Builder. Debugger. Designer of ideas.

I'm a fullstack developer who loves building things that work well — from secure authentication flows and clean APIs to minimalist creative interfaces. I use tools like Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL, React, and Tailwind to bring projects to life, always with a focus on clean structure and practical design.

I’m currently exploring frameworks like Next.js (App Router) and authentication libraries like Auth.js, while keeping my backend solid with technologies like Drizzle ORM and Prisma. I'm drawn to industry-standard practices and like understanding *why* things work, not just *how* to build them.

Outside of pure development, I have a soft spot for visual creativity — especially designing t-shirt graphics with clever or emotionally resonant phrases. I enjoy blending tech logic with expressive design to create something unexpected, whether that’s a pun in a vintage typeface or a darkly humorous twist in a vector graphic.

I'm always learning, always iterating, and always aiming to make things better — one commit (or concept sketch) at a time.
